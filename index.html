About Me

I was born in Tokyo in 1999 where I lived until my arrival to the US at 4 years old. I spent my childhood in the suburbs of Atlanta.

After high school, I chose to attend the University of Michigan's College of Engineering pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a few assorted minors.

I completed my Bachelor's on April 2021, and I am now currently a Software Engineer at Citadel in Chicago.

My current hobbies lie in golf, ukelele and distance running. These change fairly regularly.

Previous Projects

Endow

Proprietary equities trading strategies focused on providing higher EAR than traditional high yield savings accounts.


Built in collaboration with Shashank Murching, Monica Wang, Leonardo Tang and Harvey Reeves.

Sents

Sents is a tool that attempts to predict changes in securities in the market using realtime online messages scraped from all over the internet.


After much effort, it was determined that reliable alpha could not be generated with our sentiment measuring methods.


Built in collaboration with Samast Varma and Sahil Patel.
